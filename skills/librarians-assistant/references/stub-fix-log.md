## Orphan Resolution Pattern

**Insight pages often appear as orphans** because they are generated by the Zettelkasten engine and have no inbound links from manually-created concept/entities pages. The fix is to add a connection link from a topically related concept page:

1. Identify the topic/keywords of the orphan insight
2. Search `wiki/concepts/` for pages mentioning those keywords
3. Add `[[insight-slug]]` to the concept page's connections section

This works because insight pages cluster around established concepts — connecting them back to those concepts is semantically correct.
